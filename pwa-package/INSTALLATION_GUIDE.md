# 24/7 Tele H Health Monitor - Mobile App Installation Guide

## Overview
This Progressive Web App (PWA) provides professional healthcare monitoring with HC03 device integration. It installs like a native mobile app but runs through your web browser.

## What You Get
- ✅ HC03 Bluetooth device integration
- ✅ Real-time vital signs monitoring
- ✅ Patient registration and secure authentication
- ✅ Health history and analytics dashboard
- ✅ Offline functionality
- ✅ Push notifications for health alerts
- ✅ Works on Android, iPhone, and desktop

## Installation Instructions

### For Android Phones/Tablets:
1. Open **Chrome browser** on your Android device
2. Navigate to your app URL (where you deployed this package)
3. Look for "Install" button or notification banner
4. Tap **"Install"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen
6. Tap the icon to launch the app like any native app

**Alternative method for Android:**
- Tap the **3-dot menu** (⋮) in Chrome
- Select **"Add to Home screen"**
- Confirm installation

### For iPhone/iPad:
1. Open **Safari browser** on your iOS device
2. Navigate to your app URL
3. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow up)
4. Scroll down and select **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **"Add"** in top right corner
6. The app will appear on your home screen

### For Desktop (Windows/Mac/Linux):
1. Open **Chrome**, **Edge**, or **Firefox**
2. Navigate to your app URL
3. Look for **install icon** in the address bar
4. Click to install as desktop app
5. App will appear in your applications folder

## Technical Requirements
- Modern browser (Chrome 76+, Safari 13+, Firefox 79+)
- HTTPS-enabled website (required for PWA features)
- Internet connection for initial installation
- Bluetooth capability for HC03 device integration

## Troubleshooting

### Can't See Install Option?
- Ensure you're using a supported browser
- Verify the website uses HTTPS
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache if needed

### App Not Working Offline?
- Visit the app online first to cache necessary files
- Check if service worker is properly registered
- Ensure you have sufficient storage space

### HC03 Device Not Connecting?
- Enable Bluetooth on your device
- Grant location permissions (required for Bluetooth)
- Ensure HC03 device is in pairing mode
- Try refreshing the app connection

## After Installation

2. **Verify Email**: Complete email verification process
3. **Connect HC03**: Pair your health monitoring device
4. **Start Monitoring**: Begin tracking your vital signs
5. **Set Reminders**: Configure health check notifications
